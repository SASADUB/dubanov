The pursuit problem. The decision on the system of Computational Mathematics "MathCAD"

 

Describes model solutions problem of pursuit. The persecution of is produced over rough terrain. The landscape of terrain is represented by a point basis, according to which is executed polynomial regression. According to the results of mathematical modeling are created animated images.

Keywords: challenge of persecution, the trajectory of the rabbit, fox trajectory, spot basis.

В данной статье предлагаются методы решения классической задачи из школьного курса физики, описанной в замечательной книге «Задачи по физике», Библиотечка «Квант», выпуск № 5, 1980,  И.Ш.
